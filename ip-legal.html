  • Scope of confidential information and exclusions
  • Duration, survival clauses and return/destruction
  • Permitted disclosures and need-to-know perimeter

Early access · Advanced capability

End-to-end patent application preparation

From the inventor's interview to the filing-ready application, across UIBM, EPO and major national offices. Drafting, prior art search, claim generation and adversarial validation in a single workflow.

1

Structured inventor interview

Guided conversation that extracts the inventive concept, technical features and motivations from the inventor, no IP-vocabulary fluency required.

2

Prior art search

Systematic search across patent literature and technical state of the art. Closest references identified, mapped and summarised against the inventive concept.

3

Claims generation

Independent and dependent claims drafted to capture the inventive contribution with the appropriate scope, structured for both UIBM and EPO formalities.

4

Description and abstract

Full technical description with background, summary, detailed description and figure references. Abstract aligned to the granted claims.

5

Adversarial claim attack

Real attacks on each claim using prior art and existing patents, also in combination. Weak points exposed, revision guidance issued.

6

Filing green light

Approval issued only when every revision recommendation has been incorporated and the claims survive the adversarial review. No green light, no filing.
