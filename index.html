Our Mission

Building Agentic AI for legal and administrative work

We build artificial intelligence systems that transform the work of legal professionals and public administration officials, bringing within everyone's reach the kind of rigorous analysis, anchored in correct and up-to-date legal sources, that until today required the effort of dedicated specialist teams, available only in large organisations or major law firms.

TTR-SUITE per la PA Locale

Consulenza giuridica e amministrativa AI per i Comuni italiani

Mainly designed for Italian municipalities under 5,000 inhabitants and the officials who run them. Legal opinions and analytical problem-solving under current law, drafted acts ready for signature, SInCRO-compliant digital filing. Patent-pending architecture.

  • Legal opinions and analytical studies on concrete administrative problems under current law
  • 22+2 specialist agents for thematic areas and roles
  • Certified normative sources, zero citation errors
  • Drafting of acts: resolutions, determinations, ordinances, building titles
  • SInCRO-compliant digital file (UNI 11386, art. 41 CAD)
  • AI Act art. 50 compliance + GDPR by design

TTR-SUITE IP Legal

AI-powered analysis and drafting for IP legal teams and IP departments

Built for IP departments, in-house legal teams handling IP work and technology transfer offices. Now drafts and analyses NDAs, research agreements and commercial contracts with internally validated outputs. Multi-language, clause-by-clause.

  • IP analysis of bids, tenders and related documents
  • Research and technology partnership agreements
  • License agreement review with key term extraction
  • Technical presentations and papers risk assessment
  • Production-validated in the IP department of a major international corporation
  • End-to-end patent application preparation: structured inventor interview, thorough prior art search, claims drafting
  • Adversarial claim stress test, AI agents attack each claim with prior-art rewordings and combinations of known or patented techniques
